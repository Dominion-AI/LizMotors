PRODUCT REQUIRED DOCUMENT

In car manufacturing, a PRD outlines the specific features, functionalities, and performance criteria that a new vehicle model must meet. It typically includes details about the target market, technical specifications, design elements, safety standards, regulatory requirements, and other relevant considerations for the development and production of the car.

The PRD serves as a roadmap for engineers, designers, and other stakeholders involved in the development process to ensure threats the final product aligns with the company,s goals and customers expectation.

Specifications

Spec Planning in car manufacturing is the process of specifying the deatails and requirements for various components and system with in a vehicle. This includes everything from the engine and drivetrain to the interior features and electronic systems. Spec planning involves determining the specific characteristics, performance standards, and functionalities of each component system to ensure they meet the overall objectives of the vehicle design and manufacturing process.
